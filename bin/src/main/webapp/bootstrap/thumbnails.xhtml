Thumbnails

You can take advantage of BootsFaces's layout system with the thumbnail component to easily display grids of images, videos, text, and more.

Default example

By default, the BootsFaces <b:thumbnail> tag is designed to showcase linked images with minimal required markup.

EXAMPLE Markup:

Custom content

With a bit of extra markup, it's possible to add any kind of HTML content like headings, paragraphs, or buttons into thumbnails.

Just add a facet with name="caption" to the component and add your content inside.
